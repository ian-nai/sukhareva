Grunya Sukhareva

Highlighting the writings and contributions of Soviet child psychiatrist Grunya Sukhareva.



Home Sukhareva's Writings on Autism Further Reading Open Online Resources Timeline About

This website aims to make G.E. Sukhareva's work on autism research more widely known and accessible. By making information about her research and life more readily accessible to the public and academic communities, we hope to honor and highlight her scientific legacy and contribution to the history of autism.

The site was created and is maintained by Ian Goodale, European Studies Librarian at the University of Texas at Austin.
